Calculation for \(\bm|D|\text{:}\)
\begin{align*} \left|\frac{R_j(z) -R_j(z_0)}{z-z_0}\right| \amp =\left|\frac{1}{z-z_0}\left(\sum\limits_{n=j+1}^{\infty}c_n[(z-\alpha)^n-(z_0-\alpha)^n]\right) \right|\\ \amp \lt \sum\limits_{n=j+1}^{\infty}|c_n|\left|\frac{(z-\alpha )^n-(z_0-\alpha )^n}{z-z_0}\right|\text{,} \end{align*}
where the last inequality follows from Exercise 4.1.12, Section 4.1.
