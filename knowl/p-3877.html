The contribution \(\Delta \mathbf{E}_1\) induced by the element of charge \(\frac{q\Delta h}{2}\) along the segment of length \(\Delta h\) situated at a height \(h\) above the plane has magnitude \(|\Delta \mathbf{E} _1|\) given by
\begin{equation*} |\Delta \mathbf{E}_1| = \frac{(\frac{q}{2})\Delta h}{r^2+h^2}\text{.} \end{equation*}
It has the same magnitude as \(\Delta \mathbf{E}_2\) induced by the element \(\frac{q\Delta h}{2}\) located a distance \(-h\) below the plane.
