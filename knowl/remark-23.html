Remark 10.2.4.

Let \(t\) be a real number. The convention \(\mathrm{Arctan}(\pm \infty) = \pm\frac{\pi}{2}\) allows \(\mathrm{Arctan}(t)\) to denote the branch of the inverse tangent with range in \(-\frac{\pi}{2} \lt \mathrm{Arctan}(t)\lt \frac{\pi}{2}\text{.}\) With that understanding we can write the solution given in EquationÂ (10.2.4) as \(\Phi(u,v) =\frac{1}{\pi}\mathrm{Arctan}(\frac{v}{u-u_0})\text{.}\)
