Theorem 11.9.1. Nonrepeated Linear Factors.

Let \(P(s)\) be a polynomial of degree at most \(n-1\text{.}\) If \(Q(s)\) has degree \(n\text{,}\) and has distinct complex roots \(a_1,a_2,\ldots,a_n\text{,}\) then Equation (11.9.1) has the representation
\begin{equation} Y(s) = \frac{P(s)}{(s-a_1)(s-a_2) \cdots (s-a_n)} = \sum_{k=1}^n\frac{\mathrm{Res}[Y,a_k]}{s-a_k}\text{.}\tag{11.9.2} \end{equation}
