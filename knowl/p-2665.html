\begin{equation*} f(z) =\frac{1}{1-z}=-\sum\limits_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{(1-i)^{n-1}}{(z-i)^n}, \text{ valid for } |z-i| >\sqrt{2} \end{equation*}
