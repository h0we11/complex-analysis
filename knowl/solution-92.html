The inverse transformation is \(z=\frac{w-6-2i}{3-4i}\text{,}\) so if we designate the range of \(f\) as \(B\text{,}\) then
\begin{align*} w=f(z) \in B \amp \Longleftrightarrow f^{-1}(w) =z\in D_1(-1-i)\\ \amp \Longleftrightarrow \frac{w-6-2i}{3-4i}\in D_1(-1-i)\\ \amp \Longleftrightarrow \left|\frac{w-6-2i}{3-4i}+1+i\right| \lt 1.\\ \amp \Longleftrightarrow \left|\frac{w-6-2i}{3-4i}+1+i\right| |3-4i| \lt 1 \cdot |3-4i|\\ \amp \Longleftrightarrow |w-6-2i+(1+i) (3-4i)| \lt 5\\ \amp \Longleftrightarrow |w+1-3i| \lt 5\text{.} \end{align*}
