\(\cos ^3z\text{.}\) \hint{Use the trigonometric identity \(4\cos^3 z=\cos 3z+3\cos z\text{.}\)}

Arc\(\tan z\text{.}\) \hint{Choose an appropriate contour and integrate second series in Equations (7.2.8).}
(c)
\(f(z) =(z^2+1) \sin z\text{.}\)
(d)
\(f(z) =e^z\cos z\text{.}\) \hint{\(\cos z=\frac{1}{2}(e^{iz}+e^{-iz})\text{,}\) so
\begin{equation*} f(z) =\frac{1}{2}e^{(1+i)z}+\frac{1}{2}e^{(1-i)z}\text{.} \end{equation*}
Now use the Maclaurin series for \(e^z\text{.}\)}
