Using Equation (3.1.1), we have
\begin{align*} f\,'(z_0) \amp = \lim_{z \to z_0}\frac{z^3-z_0^3}{z-z_0}\\ \amp = \lim_{z \to z_0}\frac{(z-z_0) (z^2+z_0z+z_0^2)}{z-z_0}\\ \amp = \lim_{z \to z_0}(z^2+z_0z+z_0^2)\\ \amp = 3z_0^2\text{.} \end{align*}
We can drop the subscript on \(z_0\) to obtain \(f\,'(z) =3z^2\) as a general formula.
