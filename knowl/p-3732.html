The complex potential \(F(z) =(a+ib) z\) has the velocity potential and stream function of
\begin{equation*} \phi(x,y) = ax-by \text{ and } \psi(x,y) = bx+ay\text{,} \end{equation*}
respectively, and gives rise to the fluid flow defined in the entire complex plane that has a uniform parallel velocity of
\begin{equation*} \mathbf{V}(x,y) = \overline{F\,'(z)} = a-ib \end{equation*}
