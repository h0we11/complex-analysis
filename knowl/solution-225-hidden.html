For any guess \(z\) for a solution, Newton’s method gives as the next guess the number \(z-\frac{f(z)}{f\,'(z)}=\frac{z^2-1}{2z}\text{.}\) Table 4.2.2 gives the iterates, rounded to five decimal places.
