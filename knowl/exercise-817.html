Exercise 12.

Use the convolution theorem to solve the following integral equations.
(a)
\(f(t) = e^t + \int_0^te^{t-\tau}f(\tau)\,d\tau\text{.}\)
Solution.
(b)
\(f(t) + 4\int_0^t(t-\tau)f(\tau)\,d\tau = 2\text{.}\)
(c)
\(6f(t) = 2t^3 + \int_0^t(t-\tau)^3f(\tau)\,d\tau\text{.}\)
Solution.
(d)
\(f(t) = 2t + \int_0^t\sin(t-\tau)f(\tau)\,d\tau\text{.}\)
