If we treat \(u\) as a constant, this equation represents a hyperbola in the \(xy\) plane, the foci occur at the points \((\pm1,0)\text{,}\) and the transverse axis is given by \(2\sin u\text{.}\) Therefore a point \((x,y)\) on the hyperbola must satisfy the equation
\begin{equation*} 2\sin u = \sqrt{(x+1)^2+y^2} - \sqrt{(x-1)^2 + y^2} \end{equation*}
