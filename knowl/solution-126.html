Solution 2.3.16.1.

Here \(P\) and \(Q\) can be factored in the form
\begin{equation*} P(z) =(z-1-i) (z+1+i) \text{ and } Q(z) = (z-1-i)(z-1+i) \end{equation*}
so that the limit is obtained by the calculation
\begin{align*} \lim\limits_{z \to 1+i}\left(\frac{z^2-2i}{z^2-2z+2}\right) \amp =\lim\limits_{z \to 1+i}\frac{(z-1-i)(z+1+i)}{(z-1-i) (z-1+i)}\\ \amp =\lim\limits_{z \to 1+i}\left(\frac{z+1+i}{z-1+i}\right)\\ \amp =\frac{(1+i) +1+i}{(1+i) -1+i}\\ \amp =\frac{2+2i}{2i}\\ \amp =1-i\text{.} \end{align*}
