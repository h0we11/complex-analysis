Suppose that \(f(z)=u(z) +iv(z)\) and that \(z(t)=x(t) +iy(t)\) is a parametrization for the contour \(C\text{.}\) Then
\begin{align} \int_Cf(z)\,dz \amp =\int_a^bf\big(z(t)\big) z\,'(t)\,dt\notag\\ \amp = \int_a^b\left[u\big(z(t)\big) +iv\big(z(t)\big)\right] \left[ x\,'(t) +iy\,'(t) \right]dt\notag\\ \amp = \int_a^b\left[u\big(z(t)\big) x\,'(t)-v\big(z(t)\big) y\,'(t) \right]\,dt\notag\\ \amp \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad +i\int_a^b\left[v\big(z(t)\big) x\,'(t)+u\big(z(t)\big) y\,'(t) \right]dt\notag\\ \amp = \int_a^b(ux\,'-vy\,')\,dt+i\int_a^b(vx\,'+uy\,')\,dt\text{,}\tag{6.2.6} \end{align}
where Equation (6.2.6) replaced \(u\big(z(t)\big)\) with \(u, \; x\,'(t)\) with \(x\,'\text{,}\) and so on.
