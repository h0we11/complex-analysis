We can express the complex potential in \(F=\phi +i\psi\) form:
\begin{align*} F(z) \amp = re^{i\theta}+\frac{1}{r}e^{i\theta}+ia(\ln r+i\theta)\\ \amp = \left(r+\frac{1}{r}\right)\cos\theta - a\theta + i\left[\left(r-\frac{1}{r}\right)\sin\theta + a\ln r\right] \text{.} \end{align*}
