The composite function \(F_2(z) = F_1(S(z))\) determines the fluid flow in the domain \(D\text{,}\) where the complex potential is
\begin{equation*} F_2(z) = A\left(z+\frac{1}{z}\right)\text{,} \end{equation*}
where \(A>0\text{.}\) We can use polar coordinates to express \(F_2(z)\) as
\begin{equation*} F_2(z) = F_2(re^{i\theta}) = A\left(r+\frac{1}{r}\right) \cos \theta +iA\left(r-\frac{1}{r}\right) \sin \theta \end{equation*}
