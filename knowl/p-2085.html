If \(f(z)=u(x,y) +iv(x,y)\) is continuous on the contour \(C\text{,}\) then
\begin{equation} \left| \int_Cf(z)\,dz\right| \le ML\text{,}\tag{6.2.14} \end{equation}
where \(L\) is the length of the contour \(C\) and \(M\) is an upper bound for the modulus \(|f(z)|\) on \(C\text{;}\) that is, \(|f(z)| \le M\) for all \(z\in C\text{.}\)
