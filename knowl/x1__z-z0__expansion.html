Lemma 7.2.2.

If \(z, \, z_0\text{,}\) and \(\alpha\) are complex numbers with \(z \ne z_0\) and \(z \ne \alpha\text{,}\) then
\begin{align*} \frac{1}{z-z_0} \amp = \frac{1}{z-\alpha }+\frac{z_0-\alpha }{(z-\alpha)^2}+\frac{(z_0-\alpha)^2}{(z-\alpha)^3} + \cdots\\ \amp \cdots + \frac{(z_0-\alpha)^n}{(z-\alpha)^{n+1}}+\frac{1}{z-z_0}\frac{(z_0-\alpha)^{n+1}}{(z-\alpha)^{n+1}}\text{,} \end{align*}
where \(n\) is a positive integer.
