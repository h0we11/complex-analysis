Let \(\{a_n\} _{n=0}^{\infty}\) be the orbit of 0 generated by \(f_c(z) =z^2+c\text{,}\) where \(|c|\le \frac{1}{4}\text{.}\) Then
\begin{align*} a_0 \amp = 0,\\ a_1 \amp = f_c(a_0) =a_0^2+c=c,\\ a_2 \amp = f_c(a_1) =a_1^2+c, \text{ and in general, }\\ a_{n+1} \amp = f_c(a_n) =a_n^2+c\text{.} \end{align*}
