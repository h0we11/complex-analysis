We can now show why \(\int_{C}\frac{f\,'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz\) counts the number of times that \(f(C)\) winds around the origin. We parametrize \(C:z(t)\text{,}\) for \(a\le t \le b\text{,}\) and choose the appropriate branches of \(\log_{\alpha_k}w\text{,}\)giving
\begin{align*} \int_{C}\frac{f\,'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz \amp = \sum_{k=1}^n\int_{t_{k-1}}^{t_k}\frac{f\,'\big(z(t)\big)}{f\big(z(t)\big)}z\,'(t)\,dt\\ \amp = \sum_{k=1}^n \left(\log_{\alpha_k}\Big[f\big(z(t_k)\big)\Big] - \log_{\alpha_k}\Big[f\big(z(t_{k-1})\big)\Big]\right)\\ \amp =\sum\limits_{k=1}^n(\log_{\alpha_k}w_k-\log_{\alpha _k}w_{k-1})\text{.} \end{align*}
which we rewrite as
\begin{equation} \int_{C}\frac{f\,'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz = \sum_{k=1}^n[\ln \rho _k-\ln \rho _{k-1}] + i\sum_{k=1}^n\Delta \phi _k\text{.}\tag{8.7.3} \end{equation}
