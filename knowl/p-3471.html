The image of the horizontal segment \(-\frac{\pi}{2} \lt x \lt \frac{\pi}{2}, \; y=b\) is the curve in the \(w\) plane given by the parametric equations
\begin{equation*} u = \sin x\cosh b, \text{ and } v=\cos x\sinh b \end{equation*}
for \(-\frac{\pi}{2}\lt x\lt \frac{\pi}{2}\text{.}\) We rewrite them as
\begin{equation*} \sin x = \frac{u}{\cosh b}, \text{ and } \cos x = \frac{v}{\sinh b} \end{equation*}
