Exercise 12.

Suppose that \(f(z)=u(r,\theta) +iv(r,\theta)\) is analytic for all values of \(z=re^{i\theta}\text{.}\) Show that
\begin{equation*} \int_0^{2\pi}\big[u(r,\theta) \cos \theta - v(r,\theta) \sin \theta\big]\,d\theta =0\text{.} \end{equation*}
\hint{Integrate \(f\) around the circle \(C_1^+(0)\text{.}\)}
