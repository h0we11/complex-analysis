Exercise 4.

Find and illustrate the images of the following sets under the mapping \(w=z^{\frac{1}{2}}\text{.}\)
(a)
\(\{re^{i\theta}:r>1 \text{ and } \frac{\pi}{3} \lt \theta \lt \frac{\pi}{2}\}\text{.}\)
(b)
\(\{re^{i\theta}:1\lt r\lt 9 \text{ and } 0\lt \theta \lt \frac{2\pi}{3} \}\text{.}\)
(c)
\(\{re^{i\theta}:r\lt 4 \text{ and } -\pi \lt \theta \lt \frac{\pi}{2} \}\text{.}\)
(d)
The vertical line \(\{(x, y) : x=4\}\text{.}\)
(e)
The infinite strip \(\{(x, y) : 2\lt y\lt 6\}\text{.}\)
(f)
The region to the right of the parabola \(x=4-\frac{y^2}{16}\text{.}\)
\hint{Use the inverse mapping \(z=w^2\) to show that the answer is the right half-plane \(\mathrm{Re}(w) >2\text{.}\)}
