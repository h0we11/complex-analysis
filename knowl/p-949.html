The map \(w=-1+\frac{2}{z}\) (with inverse \(z=\frac{2}{w+1}\)) has
\begin{equation*} |z-1| \lt 1 \Longleftrightarrow \left|\frac{2}{w+1}-1\right| \lt 1 \Longleftrightarrow \left|\frac{2}{u+iv+1}-1\right| \lt 1 \Longleftrightarrow \left|\frac{2(u+1-iv)}{(u+1)^2+v^2}-1\right| \lt 1\text{.} \end{equation*}
