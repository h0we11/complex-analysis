Exercise 9.

Here we establish the validity of Inequality (4.4.3) in the proof of Theorem 4.4.7.
(a)
Show that
\begin{align*} \left|\frac{s^n-t^n}{s-t}\right| \amp = |s^{n-1}+s^{n-2}t+s^{n-3}t^2+\cdots +st^{n-2}+t^{n-1}|\\ \amp \le |s^{n-1}|+|s^{n-2}t| +|s^{n-3}t^2|+\cdots +|st^{n-2}| +|t^{n-1}|\text{,} \end{align*}
where \(s\) and \(t\) are arbitrary complex numbers, \(s \ne t\text{.}\)
Solution.
(b)
Explain why, in Inequality (4.4.3), \(|z-\alpha|\lt r\) and \(|z_0-\alpha|\lt r\text{.}\)
(c)
Let \(s=z-\alpha\) and \(t=z_0-\alpha\) in part (a) to establish Inequality (4.4.3).
