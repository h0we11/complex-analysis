Exercise 1.

Give a parametrization of each contour.
(a)
\(C=C_1+C_2\text{,}\) as indicated in Figure 6.2.22.
Solution.
\(C_1:z_1(t)=2e^{it}, \, 0 \le t \le \frac{\pi}{2}\text{;}\) \(C_2:z_2(t)=-t+i(2-t) \, 0 \le t \le 2\text{.}\)
(b)
\(C=C_1+C_2+C_3\text{,}\) as indicated in Figure 6.2.23.
