Exercise 12.

Verify the following identities:
(a)
\(\frac{d}{dz}C = 0\text{,}\) where \(C\) is a constant: Identity (3.1.4).
(b)
\(\frac{d}{dz}[f(z)+g(z)] = f\,'(z) + g\,'(z)\text{:}\) Identity (3.1.7).
(c)
\(\frac{d}{dz}\frac{f(z)}{g(z)} = \frac{g(z) f\,'(z) -f(z) g\,'(z)}{[g(z)]^2}\) if \(g(z) \ne 0\text{:}\) Identity (3.1.9).
(d)
\(\frac{d}{dz}\,f\big(g(z)\big) = f\,'\big(g(z)\big) g\,'(z)\text{:}\) Identity (3.1.10).
(e)
\(\frac{d}{dz}[f(z)]^n = n[f(z)]^{n-1}f\,'(z)\text{,}\) where n is a positive integer: Identity (3.1.12).
