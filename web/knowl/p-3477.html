The quantity on the right side of this equation is the difference of the distances from \((x,y)\) to \((-1,0)\) and from \((x,y)\) to \((1,0)\text{.}\) We now solve the equation for \(u\) to obtain the real part:
\begin{equation} u(x,y) = \mathrm{Arcsin}\left[\frac{\sqrt{(x+1)^2+y^2} - \sqrt{(x-1)^2+y^2}}{2}\right]\text{.}\tag{9.4.4} \end{equation}
