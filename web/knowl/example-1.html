Example 1.2.3.

If \(z_1=(3, 7)\) and \(z_2=(5, -6)\text{,}\) then
\begin{align*} z_1 + z_2 \amp =(3,7) + (5,-6) = (8, 1) \textit{{and}}\\ z_1 - z_2 \amp =(3,7) - (5,-6) = (-2,13)\text{.} \end{align*}
We can also use the notation \(z_1=3+7i\) and \(z_2=5-6i\text{:}\)
\begin{align*} z_1 + z_2 \amp =(3+7i)+(5-6i)=8+i \text{ and }\\ z_1 - z_2 \amp =(3+7i)-(5-6i)=-2+13i\text{.} \end{align*}
