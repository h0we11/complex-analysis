If \(g(\alpha) \ne 0\text{,}\) then the quotient \(\frac{f}{g}\) has the power series representation
\begin{equation*} \frac{f(z)}{g(z)} = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty}c_n(z-\alpha)^n\text{,} \end{equation*}
where the coefficients satisfy the equation
\begin{equation*} a_n = b_0c_n + \cdots + b_{n-1}c_1 + b_nc_0\text{.} \end{equation*}
