Exercise 1.

Use partial fractions to find the inverse Laplace transform of
(a)
\(Y(s) = \frac{2s+1}{s(s-1)}\text{.}\)
Solution.
(b)
\(Y(s) = \frac{2s^3-s^2+4s-6}{s^4}\text{.}\)
(c)
\(Y(s) = \frac{4s^2-6s-12}{s(s+2)(s-2)}\text{.}\)
Solution.
(d)
\(Y(s) = \frac{s^3-5s^2+6s-6}{(s-2)^4}\text{.}\)
(e)
\(Y(s) = \frac{2s^2+s+3}{(s+2)(s-1)^2}\text{.}\)
Solution.
(f)
\(Y(s) = \frac{4-s}{s^2+4s+5}\text{.}\)
