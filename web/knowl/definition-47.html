Definition 7.3.3. Annulus.

Given \(0 \le r\lt R\text{,}\) we define the annulus centered at \(\alpha\) with radii \(r\) and \(R\) by
\begin{equation*} A(\alpha ,r,R) =\{z:r\lt |z-\alpha| \lt R\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
The closed annulus centered at \(\alpha\) with radii \(r\) and \(R\) is denoted by
\begin{equation*} \overline{A}(\alpha ,r,R) =\{z:r\le |z-\alpha| \le R\} \end{equation*}
