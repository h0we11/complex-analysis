Footnote 6.2.1.

The derivatives on the right, \(x\,'(a^+)\text{,}\) and on the left, \(x\,'(b^{-})\text{,}\) are defined by the limits
\begin{equation*} x\,'(a^+)=\lim\limits_{t \to a^+}\frac{x(t)-x(a)}{t-a} \text{ and } x\,'(b^-) = \lim\limits_{t \to b^-}\frac{x(t) -x(b)}{t-b}\text{.} \end{equation*}
