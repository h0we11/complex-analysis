Suppose that \(\lim\limits_{z \to z_0}\) \(f(z) = A\) and \(\lim\limits_{z \to z_0}g(z) =B\text{.}\) Then
\begin{align} \lim\limits_{z \to z_0}\left[ f(z) \pm g(z) \right] \amp =A\pm B,\tag{2.3.4}\\ \lim\limits_{z \to z_0}f(z) g(z) \amp =AB, \text{ and }\tag{2.3.5}\\ \lim\limits_{z \to z_0}\frac{f(z)}{g(z)} \amp = \frac{A}{B}, \text{ where } B \ne 0\text{.}\tag{2.3.6} \end{align}
