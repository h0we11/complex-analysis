Example 10.11.10.

Consider the conformal mapping
\begin{equation*} w=S(z)=\frac{2}{\pi}(z^2-1)^\frac{1}{2}+\frac{2}{\pi}\mathrm{Arcsin}\left(\frac{1}{z}\right)\text{,} \end{equation*}
which maps the upper half-plane \(\mathrm{Im}(z)>0\) onto the domain consisting of the upper half-plane \(\mathrm{Im}(w)>0\) joined to the channel \(-1 \le u\le 1, \, v\le 0\text{.}\) The point \(z_0=0\) is mapped onto the point \(w_0=-i\infty\) along the channel.
