\(e^{i\frac{\pi}{2}}\text{.}\)
Solution.
(b)
\(4e^{-i\frac{\pi}{2}}\text{.}\)
(c)
\(8e^{i\frac{7\pi}{3}}\text{.}\)
Solution.
(d)
\(-2e^{i\frac{5\pi}{6}}\text{.}\)
(e)
\(2ie^{-i\frac{3\pi}{4}}\text{.}\)
Solution.
(f)
\(6e^{i\frac{2\pi}{3}}e^{i\pi}\text{.}\)
(g)
\(e^2e^{i\pi}\text{.}\)
Solution.
(h)
