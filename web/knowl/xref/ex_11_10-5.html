Exercise 5.

For flow around an infinitely long rectangular barrier with a pointed “nose,” find
(a)
the flow up an inclined step, as shown in Figure 10.10.7(a).
Solution.
\(\displaystyle w=f(z)=-1+\int_{-1}^z\frac{(\xi -1)^\frac{1}{4}}{\xi^\frac{1}{4}}\,d\xi\text{.}\) \(w=f(z)=\) \(i+\frac{1}{\pi}\left[4(z-1)^\frac{1}{4}z^\frac{3}{4} - 2\mathrm{Arctan}(1-\frac{1}{z})^\frac{1}{4} +\mathrm{Log}(1-(1-\frac{1}{z})^\frac{1}{4})-\mathrm{Log}(1+(1-\frac{1}{z})^\frac{1}{4})\right]\text{.}\)
(b)
the flow around a pointed object, as shown in Figure 10.10.7(b).
