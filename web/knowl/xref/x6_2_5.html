Exercise 5.

Evaluate \(\int_Cx\,dz\) from \(-4\) to \(4\) along the following contours, as shown in Figures 6.2.24(a) and Figure 6.2.24(b).
(a)
The polygonal path \(C\) with vertices \(-4,\,-4+4i,\,4+4i\text{,}\) and \(4\text{.}\)
Solution.
\(-32i\text{.}\)
(b)
The contour \(C\) that is the upper half of the circle \(|z|=4\text{,}\) oriented clockwise.
Solution.
\(-8\pi i\text{.}\)
