Exercise 1.

Find the electrostatic potential \(\phi(x,y)\) between the two coaxial cylinders \(r=1\) and \(r=2\) that has the boundary values shown in Figure 10.6.8:
\begin{align*} \phi(x,y) \amp = 100 \text{ when } |z|=1,\\ \phi(x,y) \amp = 200 \text{ when } |z|=2\text{.} \end{align*}
Solution.
\(\phi(x,y) = 100 + \frac{100}{\ln 2}\ln|t|\text{.}\)
