The Bessel function \(J_n(z)\) is sometimes defined by the generating function
\begin{equation*} \exp \left[ \frac{z}{2}(t-\frac{1}{t}) \right] =\sum\limits_{n= -\infty}^{\infty}J_n(z) t^n\text{.} \end{equation*}
Use the circle \(C_1^{+}(0)\) as the contour of integration and show that
\begin{equation*} J_n(z) =\frac{1}{\pi} \int_0^{\pi}\cos (n\theta-z\sin \theta)\,d\theta \end{equation*}
