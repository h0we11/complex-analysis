Theorem 5.2.7.

Let \(z_1\) and \(z_2\) be nonzero complex numbers. The multivalued function \(\log\) obeys the familiar properties of logarithms:
\begin{align} \log(z_1z_2) \amp = \log(z_1) + \log (z_2),\tag{5.2.8}\\ \log\left(\frac{z_1}{z_2}\right) \amp = \log(z_1) -\log(z_2), \text{ and }\tag{5.2.9}\\ \log\left(\frac{1}{z}\right) \amp = -\log(z)\text{.}\tag{5.2.10} \end{align}
