Exercise 5.

Show that, for all \(z\) ,
(a)
\(\sin(\pi -z) =\sin z\text{.}\)
Solution.
(b)
\(\sin(\frac{\pi}{2}-z) =\cos z\text{.}\)
(c)
\(\sinh(z+i\pi ) =-\sinh z\text{.}\)
Solution.
(d)
\(\tanh(z+i\pi ) =\tanh z\text{.}\)
(e)
\(\sin(iz) =i\sinh z\text{.}\)
Solution.
(f)
\(\cosh(iz) =\cos z\text{.}\)
