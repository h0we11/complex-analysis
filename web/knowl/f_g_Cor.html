Corollary 7.5.3.

Suppose that \(f\) and \(g\) are analytic in the domain \(D\text{,}\) where \(\alpha \in D\text{.}\) If there exists a sequence \(\{z_n\} \in D\) such that \(z_n \to \alpha\text{,}\) and \(f(z_n)=g(z_n)\) for all \(n\text{,}\) then \(f(z)=g(z)\) for all \(z \in D\text{.}\)
