Using the additive property of contours and the definition of \(F\) given in Equation (6.4.1), we have
\begin{align} F(z+\Delta z) -F(z) \amp = \int_{z_0}^{z+\Delta z}f(\xi)\,d\xi -\int_{z_0}^{z}f(\xi)\,d\xi\notag\\ \amp = \int_{\Gamma _2}f(\xi)\,d\xi -\int_{\Gamma _1}f(\xi)\,d\xi =\int_{\Gamma}f(\xi)\,d\xi\text{.}\tag{6.4.3} \end{align}
