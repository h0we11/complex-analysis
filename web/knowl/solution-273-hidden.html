Let \(z_1=-\sqrt{3}+i\) and \(z_2=-1+i\sqrt{3}\text{.}\) Then
\begin{align*} \mathrm{Log}(z_1z_2) \amp = \mathrm{Log}(-4i)\\ \amp = \ln 4+i\left(\!-\frac{\pi}{2}\right), \text{ but }\\ \mathrm{Log}(z_1) +\mathrm{Log}(z_2) \amp = \ln 2+i \frac{5\pi}{6}+\ln 2+i\frac{2\pi}{3}\\ \amp = \ln 4+i\frac{3\pi}{2}\text{.} \end{align*}
