We can use the mean value theorem for integrals with this equation to show that
\begin{equation*} p_x(x_1,y_1) + q_y(x_2,y_2) =0\text{,} \end{equation*}
where \(y\lt y_1\lt y+\Delta y\) and \(x\lt x_2\lt x+\Delta x\text{.}\) Letting \(\Delta x \to 0\) and \(\Delta y \to 0\) in this equation yields
\begin{equation} p_x(x,y) + q_y(x,y) =0\text{,}\tag{10.7.4} \end{equation}
which is called the equation of continuity.
