What are the images of rectangles under the mapping \(w=z^2\text{?}\) To find out, we use the Cartesian form
\begin{equation*} w=u+iv=f(z)=z^2 = x^2-y^2+i2xy=(x^2-y^2, 2xy) =(u, v) \end{equation*}
and the resulting system of equations
\begin{equation} u = x^2-y^2 \text{ and } v=2xy\text{.}\tag{2.2.4} \end{equation}
