Proof

If \(P(s)\) and \(Q(s)\) are polynomials and \(s_k\) is a simple zero of \(Q(s)\text{,}\) then
\begin{equation*} \mathrm{Res}[F(s) e^{st},s_k] = \lim_{s \to s_k}\frac{s-s_k}{Q(s) - Q(s_k)}P(s)e^{st} = \frac{P(s_k)}{Q'(s_k)}e^{s_kt}\text{.} \end{equation*}
This outcome allows us to write the residues in Equation (11.9.8) in the more convenient form given in Equation (11.9.9).
